FAQ

Frequently asked questions

We are open 7 days a week from April to October, 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Off-season (November to March), we are open Wednesday to Sunday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Contact us for any special requests.

During peak season (July-August), booking is strongly recommended, especially for electric bikes and tandems. Off-season, you can walk in directly, but booking guarantees availability.

Yes, a deposit of €150 is required per bike (or €300 for an electric bike). It is fully refunded when the bike is returned in good condition. We accept credit cards and cash.

Each bike comes with a basic repair kit. In case of a serious problem, call us and we will come and pick you up within a 20 km radius around Saint-Malo. This service is free of charge.

Yes! We have a key drop box outside the shop. You can park the bike, lock it and drop the key in the box. We will check the bike the next morning.

Yes, for rentals of 3 days or more, we deliver and collect the bikes for free at your hotel or accommodation in Saint-Malo Intra-Muros and Saint-Servan. A €10 surcharge applies for other areas.

Yes, we offer half-day rental (4 hours) for all our bikes and packages. Perfect for a ride along the ramparts or to Sillon beach. Half-day rates are shown in our pricing table.

Yes! Two packages with a 15% discount: the Classic Family Package (2 adult bikes + 1 child bike + helmets) and the Electric Family Package (2 e-bikes + 1 child bike + helmets). Available for half-day or full day.
