Terms and Conditions

Last updated: March 2026

Article 1 - Purpose of the contract

These terms and conditions govern the bike rental service offered by Velo Saint-Malo, located at 12 Rue de Dinan, Intra-Muros, 35400 Saint-Malo, France. They apply to all bookings made with our establishment, whether online, by phone or on-site.

The rental covers city bikes, electric bikes, mountain bikes, tandems and children's bikes, subject to availability at the time of booking.

Article 2 - Booking and cancellation

Bookings can be made:

  • Online via our website velo-saint-malo.fr
  • By phone at 02 99 12 34 56
  • On-site at our shop

Any cancellation made at least 24 hours before the rental start date is free of charge. Beyond this deadline, the full rental amount remains due.

In the event of a no-show without prior cancellation, no refund will be granted.

Article 3 - Rates and payment

Current rates are displayed on our website and in our shop. All prices are in euros and include all applicable taxes.

Payment is made on-site when collecting the bike. Accepted payment methods are:

  • Cash
  • Bank card (Visa, Mastercard)

Online payment is not available. An online booking does not constitute payment and will be confirmed by our team.

Article 4 - Security deposit

A security deposit is required when collecting the bike:

  • EUR 150 for standard bikes (city, mountain bike, tandem, children's)
  • EUR 300 for electric-assist bikes

The security deposit is fully refunded upon return of the bike, provided it is returned in good condition, without damage or missing parts (lock, helmet, basket, etc.).

If damage is found upon return, repair or replacement costs will be deducted from the security deposit. If the costs exceed the deposit amount, the difference will be invoiced to the renter.

Article 5 - Rental duration

Rentals are available in the following options:

  • Half day: 4 hours maximum
  • Full day: from opening to closing time
  • Weekend: from Friday evening to Monday morning
  • Week: 7 consecutive days

Discounts apply for longer rentals:

  • 10% discount for any rental of 3 days or more
  • 20% discount for any rental of 7 days or more

Article 6 - Renter's responsibilities

The renter agrees to:

  • Use the bike in a reasonable manner and in accordance with its intended purpose
  • Comply with the applicable traffic laws
  • Return the bike at the agreed time and in the condition in which it was provided
  • Immediately report any damage, breakdown or theft by calling 02 99 12 34 56 or by email to bonjour@velo-saint-malo.fr
  • Not lend the bike to a third party
  • Use the provided lock whenever the bike is parked

Article 7 - Insurance and liability

The renter is responsible for the rented bike for the entire duration of the rental, including in the event of theft, loss or damage, regardless of the cause.

Velo Saint-Malo does not provide insurance covering the renter. It is strongly recommended that the renter takes out personal liability insurance covering cycling, as well as theft insurance.

Velo Saint-Malo accepts no liability in the event of an accident, injury or damage caused to third parties during the rental period.

Article 8 - Delivery

For any rental of 3 days or more, Velo Saint-Malo offers a free delivery and collection service in the following areas:

  • Intra-Muros (walled city)
  • Saint-Servan

For shorter rentals or in other areas, delivery may be available for an additional fee. Please contact us for a quote.

Article 9 - Late returns

Any return made after the agreed time will be charged at the rate of one additional day. It is the renter's responsibility to inform Velo Saint-Malo of any foreseeable delay.

Article 10 - Force majeure

Velo Saint-Malo shall not be held liable for failure to perform its obligations in the event of force majeure, as defined by Article 1218 of the French Civil Code. This includes, but is not limited to, natural disasters, epidemics, general strikes or administrative decisions making it impossible to continue operations.

In the event of force majeure preventing the rental, the renter will be offered a rescheduling of the booking or a full refund.

Article 11 - Applicable law and disputes

These terms and conditions are governed by French law. In the event of a dispute relating to the interpretation or performance of these terms, the parties shall endeavour to reach an amicable resolution.

Failing an amicable agreement, any dispute shall be brought before the competent court of Saint-Malo.
